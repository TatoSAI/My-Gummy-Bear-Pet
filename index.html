How to Play

Stats to Monitor

Health: Keep your pet healthy! Low health means game over.
Hunger: Feed your pet regularly to keep them full.
Energy: Let your pet sleep when they're tired.
Happiness: Play with your pet to keep them happy!

Actions

Feed: Gives food to your pet
Play: Makes your pet happy
Clean: Remove poops from the area
Sleep: Restores energy
Medicine: Heals your pet

Tips

  • Your pet will tell you when they need something
  • Clean up poops to keep your pet healthy
  • Don't overfeed or force sleep when not needed
  • Watch out for the day/night cycle!
